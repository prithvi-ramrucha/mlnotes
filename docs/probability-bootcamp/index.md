Welcome to the probability bootcamp! These notes are based on Steven L. Brunton's YouTube series titled _"Probability Bootcamp"_ available for free at: [link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQqniayndb4&list=PLMrJAkhIeNNR3sNYvfgiKgcStwuPSts9V). The course covers the basics of probability for enginners, physicists, data scientists and machine learning practitioners. It aims to cover ground quickly in order to move to more advanced topics. I have made the effort to transcribe Brunton's short lectures into concise notes.
